Our Offerings

Excellent Learning Experience

We have come to develop a learning Model, through series of effective research and practical learning, that offers not just knowledge but an experience that internalizes this knowledge, thus transforming the kids. At SMOG Edutech our learning program is unconventional yet impactful. Our kids are made to see learning from a different approach and that is Value Creation. They are immediately armed with a mentality that positions them for thriving in this eveolving society.

Project Based Learning

In all our programs we begin with the end in mind. Our carefully designed curriculum allows for our kids to be involved in terminal projects at the end of each learning cycle. These projects help to put the learning process in perspective, giving the kids the right mentality to learning. The projects are designed to stimulate the kids mentally, push them beyond their limits and then teach them practically the skills of the 21st Century. Skills like Collaboration and creative thinking and the likes are ingrained in our kids.

Localized Learning Models

At SMOG Edutech our learning Models and learning aids are not just generic but specific to our kids. We run an adaptable learning model that can be easily adopted and bespoke to our kids at any given time. We understand the diversity in learning and learning capacity of Kids and the levels of exposure to the learning methods. With this understanding our instructors create unique models for each set of Kids or single kids we work with. We carefully learn our Kids and then help them learn in the most effective way specific to them..

Short Learning Curves

A learning curve is a correlation between a learner's performance on a task and the number of attempts or time required to complete the task. With our seasoned instructors and intentionally designed curriculum the bulk learning program is broken down into easily consumable sizes that inspire intrest and concentration. This is acheived through the creation of a sense of progress inpired by the short learning curves.
